The Tradition of Rivalry

College football is known for its rivalries, many of which have been around for over a century. These rivalrys go beyond the game; they are cultural events that divide families, towns, and even states.

The Top Conferences

  1. SEC (Southeastern Conference) - Known for its dominance and deep-south tradition.
  2. Big Ten Conference - Home to massive stadiums and historic programs.
    • Michigan Wolverines
    • Ohio State Buckeyes
    • Penn State Nittany Lions
  3. Big 12 Conference - Historically known for high-powered offenses.
  4. ACC (Atlantic Coast Conference) - Strong programs, especially in the Southeast.
